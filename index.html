About Me

Na Zheng is a Research Fellow in the School of Computing, National University of Singapore, under the supervision of Prof. Roger Zimmermann. She received her Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Shandong University, under the supervision of Prof. Liqiang Nie and Prof. Xuemeng Song. She was a Visiting Scholar of Computer Science at Texas State University (2019–2020), supervised by Dr. Yan Yan. She obtained her M.S. degree in Computer Science at Central South University. Her research interests are multimedia computing and computer vision.
