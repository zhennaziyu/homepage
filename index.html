Biography

Na Zheng is currently pursuing her Ph.D in Department of Computer Science and Technology at Shandong University, under the supervision of Prof. Liqiang Nie and Dr. Xuemeng Song. She was Visiting Scholar of Computer Science at Texas State University, in 2019-2020, under the supervision of Dr. Yan Yan. She received her M.S. in Computer Science at Central South University. Her research interests are fashion anaysis and video understanding.
