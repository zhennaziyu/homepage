About Me

Na Zheng is a Research Fellow in the School of Computing and Information Systems, Singapore Management University under the supervision of Prof. Yunshan Ma. She was the Research Fellow in the School of Computing, National University of Singapore (2022–2025), under the supervision of Prof. Roger Zimmermann. She received her Ph.D. degree (2018–2022) from the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Shandong University, under the supervision of Prof. Liqiang Nie and Prof. Xuemeng Song. She was a Visiting Scholar of Computer Science at Texas State University (2019–2020), under the supervision by Prof. Yan Yan. She obtained her M.S. degree (2015–2018) in Computer Science from Central South University. Her research interests are in multimedia computing and computer vision.
