About Me

Na Zheng is a Research Fellow in School of Computing, National University of Singapore, under the supervision of Prof. Roger Zimmermann. She received Ph.D degree in Department of Computer Science and Technology at Shandong University, under the supervision of Prof. Liqiang Nie and Prof. Xuemeng Song. She was Visiting Scholar of Computer Science at Texas State University, in 2019-2020, under the supervision of Dr. Yan Yan. She received her M.S. in Computer Science at Central South University. Her research interests are multi-media computing and computer vision.
